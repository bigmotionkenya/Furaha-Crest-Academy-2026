About Our School

Furaha Crest Academy is a learner-centered institution founded on the values of Faith, Unity, Responsibility, Academic Excellence, Holistic Growth, and Attitude of Care. We provide quality, holistic education in a safe and nurturing environment where every child is valued, supported, and inspired to achieve their full potential through a Competency-Based Education (CBE) approach.

We focus on holistic talent development, helping each learner discover and grow their unique abilities. Our dedicated staff works tirelessly to ensure that every student receives the individual attention they need to thrive academically and socially.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide a nurturing environment where students develop academically, socially, and spiritually, fostering a love for learning and a commitment to excellence through Competency-Based Education.

Our Vision

To be a leading educational institution that empowers every child to reach their full potential, building character and inspiring futures in a global community.

Our Core Values

Faith – We instill strong moral and spiritual values in our students.
Unity – We promote teamwork and a sense of community.
Responsibility – We teach accountability and integrity.
Academic Excellence – We strive for the highest standards in learning.
Holistic Growth – We nurture the whole child: mind, body, and spirit.
Attitude of Care – We show compassion and support for all.

Why Choose Furaha Crest Academy?

  • Competency-Based Education (CBE) tailored to individual learning needs.
  • Holistic development focusing on academics, sports, arts, and character building.
  • Dedicated and qualified staff providing personalized attention.
  • Safe and nurturing environment with modern facilities.
  • Emphasis on values like faith, unity, and responsibility.
  • Extracurricular activities to develop talents and leadership skills.

Our Philosophy

We believe that education extends beyond the classroom. It is about building character, instilling values, and preparing young minds to be the leaders of tomorrow.
