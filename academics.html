Academic Programs

Competency-Based Education (CBE) tailored for every stage of growth.

Playgroup & Pre-Primary (PP1 & PP2)

Our early years program focuses on play-based learning to foster curiosity and social skills.

  • Play-based learning for early development.
  • Early literacy and numeracy.
  • Social skills, creativity and values.

Lower Primary (Grades 1–3)

We build a strong foundation in literacy and numeracy while encouraging critical thinking.

  • Literacy and numeracy mastery.
  • Critical thinking and problem-solving.
  • Creativity, digital skills, and responsibility.

Upper Primary (Grades 4–6)

Students apply their knowledge and develop leadership skills through practical projects.

  • Application of knowledge and skills.
  • Leadership, teamwork and innovation.
  • Holistic talent development based on each learner’s potential.
  • Life skills and co-curricular engagement.

Assessment

Our assessment approach is continuous and practical, focusing on the learner's progress.

  • Continuous assessment and projects.
  • Practical activities and learner portfolios.

Co-Curricular Activities

We believe in nurturing the whole child. Our students participate in various activities to develop their talents.
